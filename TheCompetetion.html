NU Sailbot is a multi-disciplinary student robotics team that is designing a fully autonomous sailboat to compete in the annual International Robotic Sailing Competition. This student club is an opportunity for anyone interested in robotics, autonomy, sailing or naval architecture. SailBot is intended for students of all skill levels; from novice teams to veteran programmers and sailors. The competition includes both group and individual events for teams to demonstrate their work.
